### 📍 Key Geographic & Tactical Landmarks

* **The Accordance (Center):** Positioned at the exact heart of the diamond basin. **Midden** sits precisely at the confluence where the northern **River Vein** meets the southern **River Silvervein**. The low mountain ring provides a natural geographical shield, isolating the neutral ground from sudden blitzkriegs by the outer factions.
* **The Defensive Chokepoints:**
* The **Wyrm-Teeth** mountains separate the harsh, freezing raids of Frostmark from falling directly into the central river basin.
* The **Spine** forms a massive eastern wall containing the heavily militarized **Aethelian Empire**, forcing their expansionism to route primarily through southern valleys or heavily policed trade roads through Midden.
* The **Desiccated Plateau** to the west acts as a massive attrition buffer for **Khaz-Mora**, ensuring their dark, life-siphoning magical constructs are safe from standard land invasions.

| Faction | Primary Strategic Advantage | Geographic Vulnerability | Fatal Arcane Vulnerability |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |

| **☀️ Valeris** | Self-sustaining, highly fertile interior allows for lengthy siege endurance. | Wide, flat river valleys leave southern settlements open to mobile cavalry/artillery. | High economic dependence on centralized "sun-stone" crystal distribution channels. |
| **⚙️ Aethelian** | Massive logistical infrastructure; mobile legion-battery units allow endless casting. | Mountainous terrain limits heavy siege engine mobility from Ferrax. | Total reliance on supply lines; if gemstone shipments are cut, casters are defenseless. |
| **🏜️ Khaz-Mora** | Hostile desert terrain naturally decimates encroaching traditional militaries. | Highly centralized population hubs make their oasis cities vulnerable to blockade. | Susceptible to focus-disrupting psychological warfare (Mind's Eye disruption). |
